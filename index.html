CESS

CESS is computer engineering student society Established to enhance the skills of students. It's an organization build by computer science students. CESS stands by the motto- by the students, of the students and for the students.
CESS purpose is to help students make their journey in GNDU smooth and fruitful. It has various clubs to guide you. It enlightens the path how to enrich your life as a student and provides you with a great opportunity to network.

It consists of 8 teams -

  • Tech Team
  • Public Relations Team
  • Promotion Team
  • Placement Team
  • Literary Team
  • Design Team
  • Fine Arts Team

Fine Arts team

Cess Fine arts team is concerned with improving and encouraging creativity through visual arts.These are the arts that meet the eye and evoke an emotion through an expression of skill and imagination.

  • The visual arts are art forms that create works that are primarily visual in nature, such as drawing, painting, sculpture, crafts, photography, calligraphy, video, film making etc
  • Our goal is to organize events which develop academic skills, motor skills, visual learning, decision-making, focus, collaboration, and accountability.
  • Like every year, we’ll be arranging non-academic events that will enhance your creativity and originality.
  • Your art skills will be used and appreciated through prizes in competitions like sketch-making, calligraphy, painting, rangoli making, crafting, mehndi , origami etc.

Music Team

The Music Team aims to promote taste and talent amongst students by organizing concerts, workshops and performances. An opportunity for students to perform and showcase their talents at events throughout the year.

  • To engage musically talented/experienced students in club’s activities.
  • To maintain the progress in their musical skills throughout the academic years.
  • To bring the theoretical knowledge obtained in music courses into practice.
  • To develop organizational skills in students through creating bands and managing concerts.
  • To foster leadership skills in students through practicalities of musical performances.
  • To develop critical listening and analyzing skills, and enhance the enjoyment for music.
